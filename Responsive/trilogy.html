Polar Express Trilogy

Overview

The Polar Express (1985)

The journey begins with a young boy who, on Christmas Eve, boards a mysterious train bound for the North Pole. Led by a charismatic conductor, the Polar Express takes children on a thrilling adventure through snowy landscapes, where the true meaning of Christmas is revealed. This classic tale encourages readers to believe in the magic of the season—and in themselves. The book's hauntingly beautiful illustrations and timeless story have made it a favorite for generations, capturing the spirit of Christmas with its message of faith, wonder, and belief in the impossible.

The Polar Express: A Christmas Adventure (2004)

In this stunning 3D animated film, The Polar Express comes to life like never before. With groundbreaking visual effects and a heartwarming storyline, the movie follows the original tale, with the boy's magical train ride to the North Pole vividly depicted on screen. As he travels on the Polar Express, the boy is given a special bell as a token of his belief, setting the stage for a deeper exploration of what it means to trust in the unseen.The film’s innovative animation, powerful performances, and enchanting score elevate this adaptation to an unforgettable holiday experience

The Polar Express: The Movie Book (2004)

This special edition of the beloved Polar Express story captures all the magic of the film in a beautifully illustrated book. The Movie Book brings the film’s iconic imagery together with Chris Van Allsburg’s original narrative, offering readers a chance to relive the experience of the movie in the comfort of their own homes. With its faithful portrayal of the film’s captivating moments and a new, interactive storytelling experience, this edition is perfect for those who want to bring the holiday spirit to life.
