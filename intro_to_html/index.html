Mountain Biking

Mountain biking is a super fun way to get outside and enjoy the trails. You zip through forests, tackle rocky paths, and feel the rush as you go downhill. It’s all about the thrill and the freedom of being in nature. Plus, it’s a great workout without feeling like a chore. Whether you’re riding solo or with friends, it’s an awesome adventure!

Trails

Raccoon Mountain is a mountain biker’s paradise with a variety of trails that cater to all skill levels. The winding paths take you through stunning forests, with plenty of twists, turns, and elevation changes to keep things exciting. Some trails are smooth and flowy, perfect for beginners, while others challenge experienced riders with technical sections and steep descents. The views from the higher points are breathtaking, making it worth the climb. Whether you’re looking for a quick ride or a full day of adventure, Raccoon Mountain has something for everyone!
